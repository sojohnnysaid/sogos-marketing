The Details

What to expect

Transparency is important to us. Here's a detailed look at what working with Sogos looks like from start to finish.

Initial Consultation

Week 1

We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your business, goals, challenges, and vision. This conversation helps us determine if we're the right fit and allows us to begin forming a strategic approach. We'll discuss timelines, budget, and deliverables to ensure alignment from the start.

Research & Planning

Weeks 2-3

Deep research into your industry, competitors, and target audience. We develop user personas, map customer journeys, and create a strategic roadmap. This phase includes stakeholder interviews, content audits, and technical assessments. We present our findings and recommendations in a comprehensive strategy document.

Design Phase

Weeks 4-6

We create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes. You'll see your project come to life through iterative design reviews. We refine based on your feedback until we achieve a design that exceeds expectations. All design assets are delivered in formats ready for development.

Development

Weeks 7-10

Clean, efficient code that brings the design to life. We build with performance, security, and scalability in mind. Regular check-ins keep you informed of progress. We conduct thorough testing across devices and browsers to ensure a flawless experience for your users.

Launch & Training

Week 11

We handle the technical details of launch, from domain configuration to deployment. Comprehensive training ensures your team can manage and update your new systems confidently. We provide detailed documentation and remain available for questions as you settle in.

Ongoing Support

Ongoing

Our relationship doesn't end at launch. We offer maintenance packages, performance monitoring, and strategic consulting to help you continue growing. As your business evolves, we're here to help your digital presence evolve with it.
